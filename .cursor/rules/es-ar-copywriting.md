# es-AR Copywriting Rules (Enforceable)

## Locale and Register

- User-facing slide copy MUST remain in `es-AR`.
- Voseo forms are required for direct address (`vos podés`, `mirá`, `pensá`).
- `vosotros` and peninsular forms are disallowed.

## Tone

- Required tone: academic, precise, non-hype.
- Avoid slang, emojis, and exclamation-heavy phrasing.
- Prefer explicit connectors (`por eso`, `sin embargo`, `además`, `es decir`) for logical flow.

## Vocabulary Consistency

- Use canonical terms consistently across artifacts:
  - `inteligencia artificial`
  - `algoritmo`
  - `modelo`
  - `entrenamiento`
  - `sesgo`
  - `datos`
  - `validación`
